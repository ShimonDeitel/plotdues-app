Privacy Policy

Vendorplot - Community Garden Dues is an offline-first app. All data you enter — kind, amount, season, date — is stored only in local app storage on your device. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data, and the app makes no network requests of its own.

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple via StoreKit; we never see your payment details.

If you delete the app, your data is deleted with it. We have no servers and no way to access your data.
